Our Courses

1 Year Comprehensive Course

The 1-Year Comprehensive Course offers premium online coaching designed for focused GPAT preparation. It includes live classes, detailed e-notes, weekly assessments, mock tests, and 1-to-1 interactions with experienced educators and GPAT-qualified rankers. Sessions are conducted in English, Hindi, and Bengali to ensure complete understanding and personalized guidance.

The 2-Year Extended Course is a value-packed program that includes everything from the 1-Year Comprehensive Course, along with an additional 6 months of extended access.
